Principles in action of bilibili

Consistency

  • Understandable guidance

    When there are some new messages, a red point will appear on the certain button to remind you to check it. - When you hold your mouse to a certain button, a list of reachable operations will list out.

  • Appropriate constraints

    The mouse cursor turns to "pointing hand" when the mouse moves to a part that can be clicked, while it becomes a arrow when the part can not be clicked. The area that can not be operated will turn gery.

  • screen design and visibility.

    Clear layout. The menu, trending video section, recommended video section are separated clearly. - Nice color design. The whole page is decorated in a theme of pink and white, and the elements match in harmony.
